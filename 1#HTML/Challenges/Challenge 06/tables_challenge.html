We can use tables to lay out information for publishing purposes. Many
a times, information is required in a way that is easy to understand.
As a result, publishists find it efficient to tabulate data in a way that
makes sense to the majority of people.

Mathematics also makes use of tables. Variables are often displayed on
tables, as they are easier comprehended that way and thus, mathematicians
are able to complete certain task faster than the would if the were working
without a table.

In the scientific community, the periodic table is almost worshiped, as
it's precision allows for accurate calculations and less human error.

When it comes to data, tables are often used as a way to record values
as they provide order and naturally just make sense.

When Software is developed, tables are needed to organise various sections
of information. This ensures that there is no confusion when development
takes place

  • Mathematics
  • Periodic Table
  • Databases
